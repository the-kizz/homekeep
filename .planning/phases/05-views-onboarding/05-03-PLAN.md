<seed_to_area_mapping>
<!-- Decision per CONTEXT bottom note: "onboarding creates tasks in the Whole Home area OR in user-selected area on seed (default 'Whole Home' for common seeds). Don't auto-create areas in Phase 5." -->

Mapping strategy:
1. **Every seed defaults to the Whole Home area** (which is guaranteed to exist). This removes the need to auto-create Kitchen/Bathroom/etc. areas during onboarding.
2. The "Edit" control on a SeedTaskCard lets the user pick a different existing area from a `<select>` populated from the home's areas (fetched server-side and passed as prop). If the user wants Kitchen/Bathroom/etc. they can pre-create those areas via /h/[id]/areas before starting the wizard OR after onboarding completes.
3. The SeedTask.suggested_area field is presentational only (used for grouping in the wizard UI — "Here are some Kitchen tasks" — not for auto-routing).
